Pokemon Crystal Key Item Randomizer

Instructions

To randomize your ROM, select a Pokemon Crystal ROM file below.

The seed used to randomizer your ROM will be present in the randomized ROM's filename.

You may input a seed value greater than or equal to 0 and less than or equal to 9223372036854775807.

About

Status: alpha; there is a moderate risk of bugs and unbeatable seeds.

This randomizer shuffles around HMs and key items; for example, instead of HM01, the player might receive the OLD ROD.

The RAINBOW WING is not randomized.

PokeGear cards are not randomized.

All seeds generated by the randomizer should be beatable.

Compatibility

Currently, this randomizer supports an experimental version of crystal-speedchoice.gbc.

Other randomizations (TMs, encounters, moves, etc.) should work alongside the key item randomizations without conflict.

The following speedchoice settings are required:

NameValue
Rocket Sections Normal
Kanto Access Normal

Changes

In order to make for a more interesting randomizer experience, the randomizer patches the following changes into the game:

  • The player does not need to return the MYSTERY EGG to Professor Elm
  • The player does not need HM01 to progress through Ilex Forest
  • The player may obtain the Flower Shop item even after defeating the Sudowoodo
  • The player may take the train without having fixed the Power Plant, as long as the player has the PASS
  • The player may obtain the Cianwood Pharmacy item even if they have already returned the SECRETPOTION to Jasmine.
  • The player may take the SS Aqua without having beaten the Elite 4, on any day of the week, as long as they have the S.S. TICKET
  • The player may obtain the Pokemon Fan Club item at any time after fixing the Power Plant
  • The player may obtain the Cerulean Gym item even after fixing the Power Plant by talking to the Power Plant manager again after handing over the MACHINE PART
  • The player may obtain the Underground Warehouse item at any time during of after Team Rocket takes over the Radio Tower; during the takeover, the Radio Tower Director will have the item, and after the takeover the item will be nearby in an item ball.

Known Issues

  • It is possible to hardlock if you do not obtain all of the Goldenrdod items before triggering the Team Rocket Radio Tower takeover (in vanilla: BICYCLE, BLUE CARD, COIN CASE, and SQUIRTBOTTLE). A fix is being worked on.
  • If the player uses the BASEMENT KEY to visit the Underground Warehouse before Tean Rocket takes over the Radio Tower, the Underground Warehouse will be totally empty. There is no consequence to doing this, it's just a waste of time.
  • Some dialogs don't reveal which item was obtained; this affects the following key items:
    • BICYCLE
    • RED SCALE
    • SECRETPOTION
    • LOST ITEM
    • MYSTERY EGG
  • The player must defeat the Rocket Executives in the Goldenrod Radio Tower in the same order as in vanilla; the player cannot skip the first one and go directly to the last one.
