Pokemon Crystal Key Item Randomizer

Randomize the game's key items and HMs and see if you can beat the Elite 4.

All seeds generated by the randomizer should be beatable.

Status: alpha

Compatibility

Currently, this randomizer should support the following versions of Pokemon Crystal:

  • Pokemon - Crystal Version (UE) (V1.0) [C][!].gbc (sha1: f4cd194bdee0d04ca4eac29e09b8e4e9d818c133)
  • Pokemon - Crystal Version (UE) (V1.1) [C][!].gbc (sha1: f2f52230b536214ef7c9924f483392993e226cfb)

Changes

This randomizer shuffles around all HMs and key items; for example, instead of HM01, the player might receive the OLD ROD.

PokeGear cards are not randomized.

Additionally, in order to make for a more interesting randomizer experience, the randomizer patches the following changes into the game:

  • The player does not need to return the MYSTERY EGG to Professor Elm
  • The player does not need HM01 to progress through Ilex Forest
  • The player may obtain the Flower Shop item even after defeating the Sudowoodo
  • The player may take the train without having fixed the Power Plant, as long as the player has the PASS
  • The player may take the SS Aqua without having beaten the Elite 4, on any day of the week, as long as they have the S.S. TICKET
  • The player may obtain the Cerulean Gym item even after fixing the Power Plant by talking to the Power Plant manager again after handing over the MACHINE PART
  • The player may obtain the Pokemon Fan Club item at any time after fixing the Power Plant

Known Issues

  • If the player uses the BASEMENT KEY to visit the Underground Warehouse before defeating Team Rocket in Mahogany Town, the Underground Warehouse will be totally empty.
  • If the player uses the CARD KEY to defeat the Rocket Executives in the Radio Tower before obtaining the Goldenrod Underground item (BASEMENT KEY in vanilla), then the player **will not be able to obtain the Goldenrod Underground item**, potentially hardlocking the player from beating that file.
